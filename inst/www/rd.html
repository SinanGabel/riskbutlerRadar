Search feature

You may type one or more words e.g.: "2047" or "2047 RD" or "Annuity 2047".

You may use lower or upper case letters, it is not case sensitive i.e. "2047 RD" and "2047 rd" is the same.

The order of the words does not matter i.e. "2032 NYK" and "NYK 2032" is the same.

It is the beginning of each word that matters for the search i.e. you may search for "DK0009299133" but not "9299133". However you can search for part of a word as long as it from the beginning of the word i.e. "DK00092" will work.

If you want comparisons of two or more Bonds then these need to be included in the Bond universe, and they also need to be "similar" as described above in the Notes. As an example you can try with these two Bonds "DK0002032978" and "DK0009297350".
